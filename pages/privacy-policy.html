Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how we handle and protect your data.

Bharathi Manpower Services Pvt. Ltd. is dedicated to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of its users. We are committed to maintaining a safe and secure experience for all visitors to our website and mobile applications.

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data through Bharathi Manpower’s online platforms. It does not apply to data collected through offline channels.

Data Storage and Usage

Your information may be stored and processed in India, in compliance with applicable data protection laws. The data we collect may be shared with Bharathi Manpower’s affiliated entities, if required, to deliver services such as staffing support, recruitment, HR outsourcing, or data analysis (for example, aggregated reporting of registered users).

“Affiliated entities” refer to companies that are owned by, control, or are under common control with Bharathi Manpower Services Pvt. Ltd.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. Bharathi Manpower is not responsible for the privacy practices, security policies, or content of any external websites. We encourage users to review the privacy statements of such websites before sharing personal information.

Information We Collect

In certain areas of our website or application, we may request personal details such as your name, address, email ID, phone number, and other identifiable information. We may also collect non-identifiable demographic data like your location code, age, preferences, gender, and interests.

In addition, we may gather information about your interactions with our website, including pages visited, services accessed, IP address, browser type, access times, and referring site details. This information is used for internal analysis and service improvement purposes and is not linked to your personal identity.

Account Deletion Request

Bharathi Manpower respects your right to control your personal data. If you have created an account within our Bharathi Track (mobile application), you may request deletion of your account directly through the app by navigating to Dashboard → Profile → Delete Account.

We will process all deletion requests in accordance with applicable privacy regulations.

Location Access (For Attendance & Tracking Modules)

Our application may require access to your location for attendance and tracking functionalities:

  • Attendance Marking: When marking attendance, the app may capture your latitude and longitude (Geo Tagging) as required by your employer. Geo-fencing may also be implemented to ensure attendance is marked only within permitted areas.

Bharathi Manpower ensures that all such data is used solely for authorized work-related activities and remains protected at all times.
